How to play Hi-Lo game

1. Player enter the money in slot Low, Mid and High.

2. If place money in all slot, click on button to Roll Dice.

Player can bet in 3 ways

  • High: won if sum of 3 dices more than or equal 12.

  • Mid: won if sum of 3 dices exactly equal to 11.

  • Low: won if sum of 3 dices lower than or equal 10.

If player won by Mid, You can get 5 times reward.
